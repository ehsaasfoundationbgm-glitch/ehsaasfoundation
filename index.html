About Ehsaas Foundation

Ehsaas Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in society through charitable initiatives, education, and community development. Our mission is to serve the underprivileged and bring hope where it is needed most.

Our Projects

  • Education for All

    Providing free education and school supplies to children in need.

  • Healthcare Camps

    Organizing free health checkup camps in rural and urban areas.

  • Food Distribution

    Distributing food packets to families during crisis and emergencies.
